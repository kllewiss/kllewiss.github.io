Client
Wide Island View

Service Provided
Web design/Social media management

This webzine was created by and for a group of JET Program participants in Hiroshima, Japan. The JET Program employs native English speakers from all over the world to come to Japan and share their language and culture with Japanese youth. My greatest hurdle to overcome with this website was the amount of content displayed and how to present it in an accessible manner.
