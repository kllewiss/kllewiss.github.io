This website was created through Wordpress.org. Most of the work I did involved finding a new theme to fit the website's needs, modifying the theme to meet the demands of the site and designing different static pages.

I currently live in Hiroshima prefecture in Japan as an assistant English teacher through the Japan Exchange and Teaching (JET) program. With the spare time that I have, I volunteered to overhaul the design of the Hiroshima JET program webzine. The other foreign teachers here contribute to the content of the website and talk about various topics relevant to our jobs and life in Japan.

I refreshed the image of the website with a new logo, color scheme, and layout.

The theme I used is ionMag.

Hello, my name is Kristen
I've been living on the internet since 1994

I'm an aspiring website designer/developer based in Japan.

I graduated from the University of Florida in 2017 with a degree in Linguistics and shortly after moved to Hiroshima prefecture, Japan. Here, I've been spending my days teaching English to Japanese teenagers and then burying my nose in various coding projects to sharpen my front-end developing and designing skills.

I lean more to the design side of things, but I also love coding and have spent a considerable amount of time ensuring that I am familiar with the most common front-end languages. I'm still learning, but I think I've already come a long way!

I want my style to be fresh, modern, and easy to use. I hope you've enjoyed my first project and I look forward to hearing from ya!
