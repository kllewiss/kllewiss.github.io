About Me


I belong to the generation who experienced the beginning of the internet and grew alongside it. As a result, I feel very connected to technology and the design behind it.

I am now living in Hiroshima, Japan as an English teacher and I love it! In my down time, I'm often managing two different websites for my fellow expats, or polishing up the code on this portfolio.

However, when I'm not sharpening my skills,
I'm probably gaming with my other half and/or enjoying a nice glass of wine.

what i bring to the table

Design

My design experience ranges from digital to print. I'm familiar with standard industry software, such as Sketch, Figma, Adobe products, etc.

Empathy

Empathy is a core part of my personality. I genuinely enjoy improving the lives of others, which I think makes me a valuable addition to any team.

Coding

I have a basic skill for front-end coding. I believe that it's important for designers to understand how code works in order to better work together with developers.

Research

I have practice conducting research on various aspects of User Experience in order to build a more human-centered experience.
