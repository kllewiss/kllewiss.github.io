IC Card App

Mobile Application Concept

Here in Japan, IC cards are used by many people on a daily basis for basic commuting and travel. However, a major problem within this system is that the amount of money you have on your cards can be hard to track, as there is no mobile application or website dedicated to recharging balances or viewing expenses. I decided to create an app concept which would simplify the process of charging your cards, tracking expenses, and viewing nearby stations to assist in an easier travel experience.
