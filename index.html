  • Social media management (Instagram & Facebook advertising)
  • Loyalty cards

Background Info

In May 2015, I joined the Vine team as a front-of-house member. However, during the interview, I expressed to one of the co-owners, Teresa, that I had experience in web design and that if they wanted, I would love to have the opportunity to practice my skills by updating their online presence. At the time, it was a simple Wordpress blog with event posts, but no extensive business info, such as a menu or about page. She agreed and I was able to practice a lot of creativity and earn substantial professional experience.

I didn't expect much to come from this offer, but I feel incredibly thankful for this trust and experience that Vine gave me. As a result, I poured a lot of my energy into creating beautiful things to help their business flourish. I think the designs I created for Vine are indicative of the passion I feel for what I do and what I'm capable of when I can fully support a business that I love.

Hello, my name is Kristen

I'm an aspiring website designer/developer based in Japan.

I graduated from the University of Florida in 2017 with a degree in Linguistics and shortly after moved to Hiroshima prefecture, Japan. I lean more to the design side of things, but I also love coding and have spent a considerable amount of time ensuring that I am familiar with the most common front-end languages. I'm still learning, but I think I've already come a long way!
