Nice to meet you,
I'm Kristen Granieri!

I've been designing and playing with technology since I was tall enough to sit in a computer chair.

Although I'm currently working as a language teacher at a high school in the suburbs of Japan, I believe my experience in education reinforces a lot of the skills I've been using as a UX designer. 

As a teacher, you master observing behavior, clearly communicating directions, and fully understanding your students in order to anticipate their struggles. These skills are my strengths as a designer and a problem solver.

Along with this, I've always taken the initiative to lead, help, and create regardless of the job or title I've held in life and consider it an integral part of who I am.
