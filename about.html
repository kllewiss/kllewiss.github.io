About me

I've been designing and playing with technology since I was tall enough to sit in a computer chair.

In March 2021, I returned to the US from living and working in Japan for 3.5 years as an English teacher. While there, I discovered my main calling in design and immediately began ideating and designing products to develop my expertise. I quickly discovered that the experience I gained as a teacher worked in my favor as a designer as well.

As a teacher, you master observing behavior, clearly communicating directions, and empathizing with your students in order to anticipate their struggles. These skills are my strengths as a designer and a problem solver.
