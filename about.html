About

Hi, I’m Kristen! I’m a designer living in Gainesville, FL.

In March 2021, I returned to America from Hiroshima, Japan, where I spent the last 4 years living and working. I was a teacher at a high school in the suburbs about 40 mins. outside Hiroshima City. Teaching abroad was something I dreamt about since high school and it honestly was everything I’d hoped it would be. Enjoy a few snaps from my time there!

I'm currently working at the University of Florida as a web technician.

Since October 2021, I’ve been working with the University of Florida Information Technology (UFIT) on the web services team, where I’ve assisted developers with content design and implementation. The UFIT web services team manages over 200 sites for different departments and teams around the university, and the list is constantly growing. I have also dabbled in freelance designing and developing using Webflow, and have an example of work done for a local bakery here on my site.

Not a bad view at work! UF campus is lovely 💕

Aside from all these things, my hobbies include gaming and cooking!

I like to play MMORPGs, simulation games, and roguelites, but I’m usually open to all sorts of games! As for cooking, my favorite dish to make is either alfredo pasta or coconut curry. Since becoming vegan in 2020, I’ve had to quickly learn how to cook and do so with creativity! It’s been fun growing my culinary skills and challenging myself to recreate past favorites without any meat or dairy.

I hope you’ve enjoyed getting to know me a bit! Thanks for reading!
