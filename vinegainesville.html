Vine Sourdough Bakery

This website was created for a bakery.
They specialized in organic sourdough bread.
My goal was to create a clean, organized, and warm website to aptly display their products.

Their Main Priority

A way to showcase the product

Many people throughout Japan use an IC card on a daily basis. IC cards allow people to pay public transportation with a simple tap of a prepaid card on a scanner. Some places even allow IC cards to be used at various merchants such as convenience stores, so as long as you have your card on you, you're good to go! At their core, they sound like an amazing a daily convenience. However, one thing about them goes against that very principle. They can't be recharged online.

Introduction to Project

When I took on Vine's website, I was already a huge fan of their business

As an already established employee, I went into this project with a lot of knowledge already about Vine's business identity and established consumers, which also helped me consider their target audience.

My first task was to take pictures worthy of a brand new website.
I went in and staged photos of their entire product line to showcase on their menu.


Then, I chose and personalized a design that would fit their needs.
As the website is created through Wordpress.com, I was not able to do any coding.
Most of my work came from organizing and adding their content as well as creating their style.


Once all was completed with their website,
I regularly updated content ontheir menu, managed their social media accounts, and created print items for the store.

Personas

I knew our audience very well

I often interacted with Vine's customers and knew many of them on a first-name basis!
Vine operated within a tightly-knit community of people who were very loyal to their product.
However, I knew that there were also many people in the area who just had not yet discovered what Vine was.
