Vine Sourdough Bakery

This website was created for a bakery.
They specialized in organic sourdough bread.
My goal was to create a clean, organized, and warm website
to aptly display their products.

About the project

When I first began working with Vine,
they didn't have much of an internet presence.
They had a basic Wordpress website with a few event posts,
but no photos, menus, or anything about them as a company.


My first task was to take pictures worthy of a brand new website.
I went in and staged photos of their entire product line to showcase on their menu.


Then, I searched for a design that would fit their needs.
As the website is created through Wordpress.com, I was not able to do any coding.
Most of my work came from organizing and adding their content as well as creating their style.


Once all was completed with their website,
I regularly updated content to their menu, managed their social media accounts, and created print items for the store.

Understanding our Audience

I was fortunate to have a very insider-perspective when it came to understanding Vine’s audience.


I often interacted and knew the customers very well. Vine operated within a tightly-knit community of people who were very loyal to their product. However, I knew that there were also many people in the area who just had not yet discovered what Vine was.

My goal was to identify and create a website that would accurately inform these people about Vine’s products and ethical values in order to get them in the door!

In order to get a clearer picture I created two people to represent our target audience and better understand their needs.
