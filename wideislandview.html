About the Project

This website was created through Wordpress.org. Most of the work I did involved finding a new theme to fit the website's needs, modifying the theme to meet the demands of the site and designing different static pages.

I currently live in Hiroshima prefecture in Japan as an assistant English teacher through the Japan Exchange and Teaching (JET) program. With the spare time that I have, I volunteered to overhaul the design of the Hiroshima JET program webzine. The other foreign teachers here contribute to the content of the website and talk about various topics relevant to our jobs and life in Japan.

I refreshed the image of the website with a new logo, color scheme, and layout.

The theme I used is ionMag.
