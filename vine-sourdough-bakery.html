Vine Sourdough Bakery is an organic bakery in Gainesville, FL. They specialize in handcrafted loaves of sourdough, pastries, and fresh pasta. They needed a website that properly conveyed their brand identity and could showcase their products.

Project Type

Web design and development

Project Length

6 months

Tools Used

Figma, Webflow

The Goals

01.Create a website that matches their brand identity

Vine needed an online presence that matched a recent brand identity refresh in order to maintain consistency between their print and digital marketing materials.

02.Showcase their products and provide eCommerce functionality

The main feature of the site, as with most food service websites, is the ability to view their product! However, they also wanted to venture into new territory and have the capability to provide online ordering.
